Haikyuu Season 4 Synopsis, Release Date

Haikyuu Season 4 Synopsis, Release Thread. There is no doubt that 2019 was indeed amazing for anime fans.

In 2019, we got to see so many anime that actually come up with a good plot, some were sequels while some were brand new, it’s going to be even more exciting with a string of releases lined up.

To be honest, it’s the best time to tune yourself into anime.

Plenty on new anime make debut this year and plenty of new sequels has been announced this year. And one of the major anime that’s supposed to outrank the other anime is Haikyuu Season 4.

Recently, Twitter user Haikyuu_EN reveal the synopsis of Haikyuu season 4-

National Spring High School Volleyball Tournament, Miyagi Prefecture Preliminary Qualifiers. The Karasuno High School Volleyball Club advances to participate in the long-desired National Tournament after a fierce battle against Shiratorizawa Academy.

While in preparation for the upcoming National Tournament, the news of Kageyama’s All-Japan Youth Training Camp invitation came to them.

In addition, Tsukishima will also be invited to the Miyagi Prefecture First Year Selection Training Camp.

However, Hinata who is flustered with the difference between the 1st years makes a sudden appearance at the Miyagi Prefecture First Years’ Training Camp.

Towards the National Tournament, Hinata, Kageyama, and the Karasuno High School Volleyball Club will begin facing further challenges!!

https://twitter.com/Haikyuu_EN/status/1162979906877304832?s=20

Many fans misunderstood the announcement by official Haikyuu cast and thought the official release date for season 4 was September 22nd.

September 22nd is the date for the season 4 Kick-Off Event and not the anime itself. The official broadcast date was not revealed until August 18th.

The season 4 of Haikyuu will premiere on January 2020. Furthermore, the season 4 will release with the upcoming Haikyuu OVA Specials called “Land vs Sky” and “The Volleyball Way”.

The OVA specials will release on Blu-ray/DVD on January 22, 2020.

The trailer is one minute long and does not reveal much about the new season.

It starts with the recap of the key moments from the previous seasons notably the last smash of Hinata which led Karasuno to victory against Shiratorizawa.

They gave a hint towards the update of character design of Hinata and Kageyama through an illustration in the latter part of the trailer.

The trailer also had two OSTs Just 1 Point and The Battle Of Concepts to provide a nostalgic feel to the fans.
