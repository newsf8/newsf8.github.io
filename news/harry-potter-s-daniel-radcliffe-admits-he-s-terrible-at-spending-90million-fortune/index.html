Harry Potter's Daniel Radcliffe admits he’s 'terrible' at spending £90million fortune

It’s one of life’s nicer problems to have, but Harry Potter star Daniel Radcliffe admits he is “terrible” at being rich. The 30-year-old actor, who is estimated to be worth £90million, says he’s bad at spending – as his pals often tell him. “I don’t do a huge amount with my money,” he admits. “I’m not particularly extravagant. There are moments where I think, ‘Man, I’m really bad at being famous.’” At the suggestion he’s also bad at being rich, he tells James O’Brien’s podcast Full Disclosure: “I’m terrible.” But co-star Rupert Grint, who played Ron Weasley, sounds excellent at spending his cash, splurging on a hovercraft, a swan-shaped pedalo and an ice cream van. (Image: PA) Daniel admits he felt “lonely” after Harry Potter ended. He is working on a script, on the advice of Gary Oldman, who played Sirius Black. “He was hugely influential,” he says. The actor’s stash has built up since he first played Potter back in 2001, aged 11. Eight movies later, Daniel is now one of the most recognisable people on the planet With his fortunes largely unspent, he has been able to focus on working on indie films like The Woman In Black, Kill Your Darlings and Horns. (Image: Warner Bros) (Image: BBC/Wall to Wall Media Ltd/Stephen Perry) Daniel hilariously revealed he was mistaken for a homeless person in New York as he sat on the floor outside with his dog. The actor was waiting for his girlfriend who was in a shop when he knelt down on the floor with the dog his girlfriend was fostering, trying to keep it warm. A kind stranger approached him and gave him $5, urging him to buy himself a coffee. (Image: BBC/Wall to Wall Media Ltd/Marcia Gresham) Daniel, who is usually recognised due to his huge fame, told the story on the Graham Norton Show. "I saw this guy look at me and smile," he said. "Then he walked past me, got about five steps away, reappeared with a five dollar bill over my shoulder and went 'get yourself a coffee, mate'." (Image: BBC) "Apparently I have to shave more often!" he joked. Daniel recently spend time learning about his family history and went on a very emotional journey on Who Do You Think You Are? after he discovered his great-grandfather's suicide note . He also uncovered photo of his great, great grandfather Louis, who he looks strikingly similar to.
