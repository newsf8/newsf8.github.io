What’s your beauty pamper ritual?

My beauty pamper is simple. Every morning, I wash my face and apply moisturiser. My night ritual includes applying a sheet mask, a clay mask or my green tea sleeping mask.

Describe your personal style.

My personal style is comfortable and laidback. I look best in whatever I’m comfortable in. Even when I experiment, I always look for comfort. If I’m not comfortable I’ll keep fidgeting. Comfort comes first for me.

Ever had a beauty emergency before a big event / shoot? How did you deal with it?

It was the trailer launch of my first movie Dabangg 3. I didn’t have my outfit ready until an hour before the event. I ended up calling my friend’s mother, who’s a designer. Luckily, she had this pretty, pink outfit ready, which I finally wore. (Smiles) I looked pretty.

Do you follow any detox or cleansing diets?

I don’t follow any detox or cleansing diets, as such. My daily lifestyle consists of eating healthy, mainly ghar ka khana. A detox or cleanse happens for me every Tuesday because I eat only fruits that day. I’ve been doing it since I was young.

Who’s your go-to designer and why?

My go-to designer is Ashley Rebello. He knows exactly what looks good on me and what I’d be comfortable in. He’s sweet and I love working with him.

One styling hack that has changed your life…

I learnt to start visualising a whole outfit together. Earlier, I’d pick up random separates and pair them together. But now that I’ve started working with stylists, I like how they plan an outfit from head to toe.

A make-up hack you learned on the job?

Applying blush on my ears to make my face look softer overall.

A RED-CARPET fashion choice you regret…

I haven’t been on enough red carpets to regret any choice. But maybe in a few years when I look back, I might have a few regrets.

Fashion and beauty are important to your profession. do you sometimes get tired of keeping up?

I do get tired of keeping up sometimes. At times, I like to keep it casual with pyjamas and a messy bun, no make-up and just a lip balm. I like to visit a restaurant looking like that. But as with every job, this one comes with a few sacrifices. I’m okay with these sacrifices because this job is worth everything.

The most stylish actor and actress in Bollywood…

Ranveer Singh and his female counterpart; the beautiful Deepika Padukone.

One person whose wardrobe you would like to raid?

Alia Bhatt’s for sure. She’s comfortable in her own skin. I love what she wears because it always suits her. I’d love to have her clothes.

Your all-time fashion icon…

Grace Kelly.

One trend you want to try…

I’ve seen this trend online where people wear bandanas as tops. It looks chic and stylish.

A colour that looks best on you…

Black looks best on me, so do bright pink and yellow.

One fashion myth you’d like to bust for the girls/boys?

Wear what you want. It’s not like if you’re a girl you have to wear a dress; if you want to wear a suit, wear it and own it. If you’re a guy and want to paint your nails pink, do it. Stop following gender norms.

Your ideal dress for a date night…

Either a white lace dress or a polka dot dress. I like to keep it classic.

What would you like to see your beau wearing for the first date night?

A white linen, beachy shirt and a nice pair of jeans.

Who according to you pulls off a saree best?

Sushmita Sen and Priyanka Chopra, both are so elegant.

Back stilettos or red pumps?

Black stilettos, always.

Who according to you pulls off the best red lips in Bollywood?

Aishwarya Rai Bachchan looks the best in a red lip. I don’t think anyone can rock a red lip like her. Especially, after seeing how beautiful she looked in Ae Dil Hai Mushkil, I’ve become a huge fan of the red lip trend.

A trend you’re seen wearing the most…

I’m into cargo pants. That’s what you’re going to be seeing me wearing the most paired with a crop top.

You favourite off-duty outfit…

My mom’s pyjamas paired with my dad’s t-shirt.

Three words that describe your personal style.

Comfortable, casual and chic.

“Celebrity yoga expert Shrradha Setalvad celebrates International Yoga Day with ‘One World Yoga Anthem’, starring Juhi Chawla, Madhoo Shah, Bhagyashree and Saiee Manjrekar”

New Delhi [India], June 19 (ANI/Newswire): On the 6th International Yoga day, treat yourself to a visual delight and celebrate with ‘One World Yoga Anthem’. In an effort to extend support to the Prime Minister’s vision of unifying the world with yoga, the Yoga Institute of India, in collaboration with celebrity yoga expert Shrradha Setalvad, will be celebrating International Yoga Day with ‘One World Yoga Anthem’.

Celebrating this iconic day with spiritual and patriotic fervor are a number of Bollywood superstars as well, the likes of which include Juhi Chawla, Madhoo Shah, Bhagyashree and Saiee Manjerekar, among others.

The video unites yoga enthusiasts from all over and also features internationally acclaimed yogis. The video also pays homage to the frontline warriors, the doctors, policemen, and essential service providers, who have been working tirelessly to get us through the pandemic of coronavirus.

Taking forward the honorable Prime Minister, Narendra Modiji’s vision of International Yoga Day, Manju Mangal Prabhat Lodha, wife of BJP President, Mumbai, Mangal Prabhat Lodha has forayed a powerful spiritual awakening for the country through the video.

Numerous notable personalities have acknowledged and praised Shraddha’s unique and inspirational initiative. Amruta Fadnavis, Indian banker, singer and Social activist, and wife of former Chief Minister, Devendra Fadnavis, has also extended her support to this cause. Padma Shri Awardee, Dr Mohsin Wali, former physician to the President of India, has praised Shrradha’s vision.

The video which is sure to inspire viewers to dedicate time and energy to this rejuvenating ancient practice will have a Global Digital Launch via Facebook Live and YouTube Live on Sunday, 21st June 2020 at 10.05 am, on Times Music Spiritual channels.
