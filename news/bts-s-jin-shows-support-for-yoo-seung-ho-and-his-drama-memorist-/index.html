BTS’s Jin Shows Support For Yoo Seung Ho And His Drama “Memorist”

BTS’s Jin cheered on Yoo Seung Ho for his new drama with a thoughtful gift!

On March 26, Yoo Seung Ho uploaded a photo of him posing in front of a coffee truck gifted by Jin. The large banner reads, “World star BTS’s Jin dedicates this to the great actor Yoo Seung Ho.” In his Instagram post, Yoo Seung Ho also wrote, “To world star Jin, I’ll enjoy the coffee. From great actor Yoo Seung Ho.”

Currently, Yoo Seung Ho is starring in the tvN Wednesday-Thursday drama “Memorist.” The new drama is based on a webtoon of the same name and tells the story of Dong Baek (Yoo Seung Ho), a detective with supernatural powers, and Han Sun Mi (Lee Se Young), an elite criminal profiler, who team up to track down a brutal serial killer.

Yoo Seung Ho Promises High-Quality Action Scenes As He Transforms Into A Detective In “Memorist”

Upcoming drama “Memorist” is hyping up the excitement for its premiere with new stills of Yoo Seung Ho!

Based on the webtoon of the same name, the tvN drama is about a detective with supernatural powers named Dong Baek (played by Yoo Seung Ho) and an elite criminal profiler named Han Sun Mi (played by Lee Se Young) who work together to track down a serial killer.

The newly released stills show Yoo Seung Ho as Dong Baek, a modern-day hero with the ability to scan other people’s memories. In one photo, Dong Baek is connected to various wires as scientists study his supernatural power. In another photo, Dong Baek hunts down criminals with keen determination in his eyes.

Yoo Seung Ho commented, “There’s a lot of action in this project. It was my first time doing action stunts, so I practiced a lot since two months ago. I will show refreshing yet intense action.”

The production crew shared, “From the first episode, detective Dong Baek with super powers will play a powerful role. His method of investigation, which includes his ability to scan memories and feel the victims’ emotions, will provide refreshing [entertainment].”

“Memorist” premieres on March 11 at 10:50 p.m. KST and will be available with English subtitles on Viki!

Yoo Seung Ho And “Memorist” CP Respond To Comments About His Looks In The Drama

Yoo Seung Ho garnered attention for his bulked up appearance in “Memorist.”

Since the drama’s premiere on March 11, some viewers have been posting comments about the actor’s appearance, saying that he seems to have gained weight. Through his Instagram story, he explained, “I purposely gained a lot of weight because I’m playing a police officer. I know that my face got plump too.” He deleted the story later.

The production crew responded to comments about his weight gain as well, saying, “Before filming, he suggested [bulking up his body] for higher synchronization [with his character].”

Chief producer (CP) So Jae Hyun commented, “Actor Yoo Seung Ho intentionally bulked up through exercise to portray detective Dong Baek from the the original webtoon. At the preliminary meeting before going into filming, he was worried about whether he would be similar to his character and made the proposal himself, and the production crew agreed with it. The production crew is very grateful for his professional mindset as an actor.”

The second episode of “Memorist” will air on March 12 at 10:30 p.m. KST.
