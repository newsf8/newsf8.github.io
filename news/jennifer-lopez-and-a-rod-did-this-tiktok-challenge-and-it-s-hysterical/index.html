Jennifer Lopez and A-Rod did this TikTok challenge and it’s hysterical

Alright, let us break it down for the boomers and Gen X-ers; formally known as musical.ly, TikTok is a social media app where users create and share videos; usually music-related, although it has since featured make-up tutorials and day-to-day documentation, with ex-Viners using it for their funny skits. The latest challenge to take TikTok by storm is the Flip The Switch challenge, where two people dance in front of a mirror to Drake’s Nonstop and literally flip the switch (as in, turn the lights off and on) when Drake sings “I just flipped the switch.” When the light comes back on, the dancing duo have swapped clothes. The latest people to join in the TikTok fun are JLo and fiancé Alex Rodriguez, who absolutely slayed. Senator Elizabeth Warren and Saturday Night Live comedian Kate McKinnon also gave it a go.
