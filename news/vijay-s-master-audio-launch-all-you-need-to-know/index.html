Vijay's Master audio launch: all you need to know

Thalapathy Vijay’s fans have geared for the grand audio launch event of his upcoming film, Master.

The audio launch event of Master will be held on Sunday at Leela Palace, Chennai, from 6.30pm onwards. Vijay’s speech is unarguably the most anticipated part of the Master audio launch event.

Due to the Coronavirus outbreak, the makers of Master have decided to keep the audio launch in the presence of the cast and crew. Sources close to the team said that they have not invited fans and press to avoid public gathering due to Coronavirus outbreak.

As per the official announcement, the event will have a live telecast on Sun TV.

So far, the makers have released three songs - Kutty story, Vaathi coming and Vaathi raid, which have become chartbusters. Four more songs, composed by Anirudh Ravichander, will be unveiled at the audio launch.

Vijay has been maintaining a dignified silence over the consecutive IT raids that have happened at his residences and office. Fans are waiting to hear his speech and all the more eager to know if the actor might finally break his silence over the same.

If the reports are to be believed, Vijay might finally open up about his highly anticipated political entry at the Master audio launch event. The actor, who usually prefers to stay away from openly discussing his political ideologies, might finally clear the air put an end to the ongoing speculations through his speech at the event.
