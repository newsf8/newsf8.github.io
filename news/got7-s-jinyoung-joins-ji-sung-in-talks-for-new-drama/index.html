“Jinyoung GOT7 dan Ji Sung Bakal Bintangi Drama Devil Judge”

Jakarta, CNN Indonesia –

Park Jin-young dari GOT7 akan kembali bermain drama Korea. Kali ini, ia akan beradu akting dengan aktor Ji Sung drama Devil Judge.

“Jin-young mendapat tawaran bermain dalam Devil Judge dan saat ini kemungkinan akan menerima peran tersebut,” demikian pernyataan agensi Jin-young, JYP Entertainment, seperti dilansir Newsen via Naver, Kamis (13/8).

Devil Judge akan mengisahkan hakim kepala yang mengubah pengadilan menjadi reality show untuk menghukum pelaku kejahatan. Sementara itu, hakim lain sedang berusaha untuk mengetahui identitas hakim kepala yang sesungguhnya.

Ini merupakan drama kedua yang dibintangi Jin-young tahun ini. Sebelumnya, ia juga membintangi drama romantis When My Love Blooms dan berperan sebagai Han Jae-hyun versi muda.

Pada 2016, ia juga sempat menjadi Lee Min-ho versi muda dalam drama The Legend of the Blue Sea.

Jin-young GOT7 resmi memulai karier sebagai aktor pada 2012 dengan membintangi Dream High Season 2 bersama Jaebum GOT7. Kala itu, mereka debut dalam grup JJ Project.

Setelah itu, Jin-young sempat membintangi beberapa drama romansa lainnya, seperti When a Man Loves, School of Magic, serta sedikit bumbu crime dalam He is Psychometric.

Sementara itu, Devil Judge akan menjadi drama terbaru Ji Sung pada 2020. Ia terakhir kali bermain dalam Doctor John yang tayang pada 2019.

Suami dari aktris Lee Bo-young ini sudah menarik perhatian melalui sejumlah drama, seperti Secret Love (2013), Kill Me Heal Me (2015), Entertainer (2016), Defendant (2017), dan Familiar Wife (2018).

Sejak debut pada 1999 sebagai aktor, Ji Sung juga sudah bermain dalam beberapa film, seperti Whistling Princess (2002), Whatcha Wearing? (2012), Confession (2014), dan Feng Shui (2018).

“GOT7’s Jinyoung, Han Hyo Joo, Park Shin Hye, And More Donate Toward Flood Relief Efforts”

More celebrities are donating to aid those affected by Korea’s ongoing heavy rains and flooding.

After Korea’s rainy season started in June, torrential rains have been inundating the country in record amounts. These monsoon rains concentrated in Korea’s central and southern regions have flooded residential areas, causing thousands to evacuate.

On August 6, the Korea Disaster Relief Association revealed that GOT7’s Jinyoung quietly made a donation of 20 million won (approximately $16,860) to help the victims of flooding. Newsen reported that Jinyoung did not inform his company about the donation.

It was revealed on August 10 that Han Hyo Joo would be donating the total amount earned from her appearance on the August 9 episode of tvN’s variety show “Hometown Flex” to the New Life Support Center located in Chungju. At the time of the broadcast, Han Hyo Joo’s hometown Chungju was seeing damage due to the heavy rainfall.

Park Shin Hye also made a generous donation, contributing 100 million won (approximately $84,300) through the Korea Disaster Relief Association to aid the flood victims.

Furthermore, News1 reported that actress Park Bo Young donated 20 million won to the Korea Disaster Relief Association as well.

Chun Woo Hee was reported to have donated 10 million won (approximately $8,430) to the same association on August 7. The donation will be used to support flood victims and to restore damaged areas.

On August 6, Yoon Se Ah revealed on her Instagram account that she also made a donation of 10 million won to the Korea Disaster Relief Association and wrote, “We’re in this together. Stay strong!”

“After Park Seo Joon, Park Shin Hye gives 100 million won for Korea flood relief; GOT7’s Jinyoung donates too”

Park Shin Hye and GOT7 member Jinyoung join the list of celebrities who contributed towards the relief funds aiding those impacted by the South Korea floods.

More and more South Korean celebrities are stepping forward to contribute towards relief funds aiding those affected by the floods in the country. Yonhap reported that, as of August 11, at least 42 people have died or are missing while more than 7,500 people have been displaced due to the ongoing monsoon and flood situation in the country. While several regions of the country continue to battle the rainfall, it is reported that #ALIVE actress Park Shin Hye has donated towards relief funds.

Via Soompi, Donga revealed The Heirs star has donated 100 million won (approximately $84,300) to Hope Bridge National Disaster Relief Association. It was previously reported that Itaewon Class star Park Seo Joon also made a similar donation towards the Korea Disaster Relief Association earlier this month.

Apart from Park Seo Joon and Park Shin Hye, Newsen reported that GOT7 member Jinyoung has also donated towards the funds for flood victims. Jinyoung quietly donated 20 million won (approximately $16,860) to aid the victims of flooding. The international news publication revealed Jinyoung kept the donation a secret even from his company.

Actress Park Bo Young has reportedly donated 20 million won to the association as well. Chun Woo Hee donated 10 million won (approximately $8,430) whereas Yoon Se Ah revealed via Instagram that she donated 10 million won for the relief as well. It is reported that the donation will be used to support flood victims and restore damaged areas.
