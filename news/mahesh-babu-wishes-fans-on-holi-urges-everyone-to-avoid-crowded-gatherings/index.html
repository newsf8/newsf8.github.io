Mahesh Babu wishes fans on Holi, urges everyone to avoid crowded gatherings

Telugu superstar wished a joyful Holi to his fans on Tuesday but also added a word of caution for his fans amid coronavirus outbreak. The 44-year-old actor urged everyone to avoid crowd gatherings and play safe. The festival of colours has been majorly affected due to the outbreak of the deadly virus (Covid-19) in the country with most people avoiding to celebrate in crowded places.
