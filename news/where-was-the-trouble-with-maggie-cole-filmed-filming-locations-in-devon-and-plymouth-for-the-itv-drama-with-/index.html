The new ITV drama centres around Dawn’s eponymous character Maggie, the self-appointed oracle of a close-knit community in the coastal town of Thurlbury who finds herself in trouble when a radio interview goes very wrong.

She is flattered when a journalist interviews her about local life, and ends up giving him far more detail about the locals and their personal lives than anyone had bargained for. In the aftermath of the broadcast, Maggie gets into trouble as the fallout from the interview disrupts the lives of her fellow residents.

Where was The Trouble With Maggie Cole filmed?

Filming primarily took place in various locations across Devon, including Burgh Island, a tidal island on the coast of South Devon. However, areas in Cornwall and Plymouth were also used.

Cornwall

French shared a picture of herself filming scenes in Cornwall, in particular at Launceston Castle.

Plymouth

Various locations including Cargreen, Noss Mayo and Erme Primary School feature in the drama series.

In fact, a group of children from the Plymkids Theatre Company in Plymouth star in the show as students at Thurlbury Primary School, including nine-year-old Jamie Talbot, who takes on the role of Tommy Jarvis.
