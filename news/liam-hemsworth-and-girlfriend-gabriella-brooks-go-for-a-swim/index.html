Liam Hemsworth and girlfriend Gabriella Brooks go for a swim

Liam Hemsworth and Gabriella Brooks started their day off right with a beach swim in Byron Bay (Picture: Splash News) It seems like Liam Hemsworth and his girlfriend Gabriella Brooks are big fans of the mantra, ‘couples that beach together, stay together’. While we may have just made that one up, there’s no denying that Liam, 30, and his gal are big beach-goers and they were recently seen starting their day off right with a morning swim. Heading out in Byron Bay, the good-looking couple stripped down to their swimwear to take a dip together and the results were pretty much reminiscent of a scene from James Bond. We’re talking Liam stepping out of the water all brooding and muscly, while his very own Bond girl, Gabriella, 21, is right beside him rocking her black bikini. 007 eat your heart out. After their swim, the couple were seen wrapping up in their towels as they took a stroll along the beach.
