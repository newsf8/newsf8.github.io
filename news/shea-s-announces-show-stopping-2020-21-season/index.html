Shea’s announces show-stopping 2020-21 season

BUFFALO, N.Y. (WKBW) — Get ready for a show-stopping, toe-tapping season at Shea’s Performing Arts Center in Buffalo.

The 2020-21 season, which was announced Tuesday evening, will include To Kill a Mockingbird, Tootsie, Pretty Woman: The Musical, Oklahoma! My Fair Lady, Ain’t Too Proud and Disney’s Frozen.

The M&T BANK 2020-21 BROADWAY SERIES:

To Kill a Mockingbird (National Tour Launches at Shea’s)

August 15-22, 2020

October 3-10, 2020

Pretty Woman: The Musical

Oklahoma!

My Fair Lady

March 23-28, 2021

Disney’s Frozen
