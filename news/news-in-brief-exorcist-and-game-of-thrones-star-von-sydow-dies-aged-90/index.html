News in brief: Exorcist and Game Of Thrones star Von Sydow dies aged 90

MAX VON SYDOW, star of more than 160 films and TV shows, has died aged 90. The Swedish-born actor (pictured), who became a French citizen in 2002, died on Sunday in France. He was known as a priest in The Exorcist and a knight in Ingmar Bergman’s art house classic The Seventh Seal. He also starred in 1994’s Judge Dredd and as the Three-Eyed Raven in Game of Thrones. Neil Crawford, project manager of architect Studio E, told the inquiry into the fire that sales literature used by manufacturer Celotex exploited their limited understanding of information about its RS5000 insulation. Combined with Reynobond PE panels, the insulation made up the external cladding system, which was a key factor in the fire’s rapid spread by acting as a fuel source.
