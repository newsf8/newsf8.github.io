Mahesh Babu extends his Holi wishes; urges everyone to avoid gatherings and play safe

Wishing you a Holi full of joy and happiness Please avoid crowd gatherings and play safe. #HappyHoli #Colortheworldwithlove❤ — Mahesh Babu (@urstrulyMahesh) 1583746660000 On the occasion of Holi, Telugu superstar Mahesh Babu took to his official social handle and wished a Happy Holi full of joy and happiness. Amid coronavirus outbreak, the 44-year-old actor urged everyone to avoid crowd gatherings and play safe. Meanwhile, the festival of colours has been hit hard by the deadly virus (Covid-19) in the country with people feeling scared to celebrate in crowded places.Recently, Mahesh Babu achieved a big milestone on social media by emerging as the only South Indian actor with 9M Twitterati in his kitty. He managed to beat the likes of Kollywood star Dhanush (8.9M), Samantha (7.8M), Shruti Haasan (7.5M), Mohanlal (5.9M) and Nagarjuna (5.9M).Unlike the early years, the Sarileru Neekevvaru star is staying pretty active on social media. Besides updating about his professional commitments, he is treating his followers and fans by sharing special moments from his personal space with wife Namrata Shirodkar and kids, Gautham and Sitara.On the work front, speculations are rife that Mahesh has agreed to play an extended cameo in Chiranjeevi-Koratala Siva’s film titled as Acharya . An official confirmation is awaited by the makers.
