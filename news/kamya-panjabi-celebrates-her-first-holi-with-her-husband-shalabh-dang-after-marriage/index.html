Kamya Panjabi Celebrates Her First Holi With Her Husband, Shalabh Dang After Marriage

The festival of colours, Holi brings back the zillions memories from our childhood. It was no less than war as our one wrong step can make us the target of a water balloon fight. It was a perfect time to gang up and create a team with the best shooters and planners, who would leave no chance to drench the enemy with water balloons, colours and pichkari. Ahh, those were the best days. But it is the first first Holi celebrated with our 'better-half' that holds a special place in our heart. And television actress, Kamya Panjabi turned a kid as she enjoyed her first Holi after marriage with her husband, Shalabh Dang.
