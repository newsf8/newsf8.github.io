Radio DJ Stevie G will be keeping spirits up on St Patrick’s Day by hosting online disco broadcasts across a range of social media platforms.

The Red FM DJ, who also performs in venues around Cork on a regular basis, will be performing a special broadcast for kids that will feature “all the pop stuff and things that they like, the tracks that will get on older people’s nerves”.

He told Neil Prendeville this morning that he is “bringing the party to the people” because the people can’t come to the party.

You can tune in by following Stevie G on Facebook and Twitter, where he will give further details on how to catch the special shows. Catch Stevie G’s gig on his Facebook page

And on Wednesday, a star-studded cast of musicians and performers will take to Facebook Live for a unique concert.

Cork-based company Unemployable Promotions have organised an evening of great original music from a host of Corkonian and Irish stars.

You can access the full event details here, including how to tune in on the night, with the performance starting at 6pm.

Emma Langford, Sara Ryan, Jordan Run, Beyond The Wash, Paddy Dennehy, Eve Clague, Mide Houlihan and Dylan Howe (Rowan) will all be performing online, and for those who wish to support the musicians you can donate here.
