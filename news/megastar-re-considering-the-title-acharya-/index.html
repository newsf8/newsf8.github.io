Megastar Re-Considering The Title Acharya?

(Image source from: Ntvtelugu.com) Megastar Re-Considering The Title Acharya?:- Megastar Chiranjeevi after the super success of Syeraa started shooting for his next film titled Acharya. Top director Koratala Siva is directing this social drama which has a message along with all the commercial elements. The shoot of the film is taking place in Kokapet in a special set erected for the film. Trisha is the heroine and Superstar Mahesh Babu will be seen playing a crucial role. Chiranjeevi during an event recently announced the film's title Acharya. Koratala Siva wanted a title change for the film and got a nod from Chiranjeevi. With the addition of Mahesh Babu, a new title will be finalized and will be announced. Mahesh Babu will be seen during the flashback episodes and he will have screentime of 30 minutes. The title will be announced officially soon. Konidela Production Company and Matinee Entertainments are the producers. The film releases on August 14th this year.
