Chris D’Elia, an actor and comedian from New Jersey, is under scrutiny following allegations of misconduct involving a teenage girl that surfaced Tuesday night.

A woman has accused D’Elia of making advances toward her in 2014 and 2015, when she was 16 and he was 34.

Simone Rossi posted screenshots of emails she says D’Elia sent her as part of a Twitter thread about the alleged incident.

Rossi started out by saying that she couldn’t believe D’Elia had been cast as a pedophile in the Netflix series “You.” In the show, D’Elia plays Joshua “Henderson” Bunter, a comedian who is secretly a child molester.

“Like the literal IRONY,” Rossi tweeted.

D’Elia was also cast as a pedophile in a 2011 episode of “Workaholics,” a sitcom on Comedy Central. The episode’s title: “To Friend A Predator."

Rossi’s screenshots appear to show messages from July 2014 and January 2015. In the emails, D’Elia asks for a picture of Rossi. She responds by sending him a photo of a panda bear.

“I’m in Tempe,” D’Elia says in a message to Rossi from 2015. “Let’s hang.”

Rossi said D’Elia was aware she was in high school at the time because her Instagram account, which he had asked to see and later said he was following, was filled with “high school football games and spirit days.”

“I am also definitely not the only underage girl he did this too,” Rossi continued. “Just an FYI.”

D’Elia issued a statement to People Wednesday.

“I know I have said and done things that might have offended people during my career, but I have never knowingly pursued any underage women at any point, he said. “All of my relationships have been both legal and consensual and I have never met or exchanged any inappropriate photos with the people who have tweeted about me. That being said, I really am truly sorry. I was a dumb guy who ABSOLUTELY let myself get caught up in my lifestyle. That’s MY fault. I own it. I’ve been reflecting on this for some time now and I promise I will continue to do better.”

In one tweet, D’Elia was accused of soliciting nude photos from minors and trying to make sexual advances toward a 16-year-old. In another, D’Elia was accused of inviting an underage fan to “cuddle” with him in his hotel room when he was on tour.

Rossi talked about why she wanted to come forward with the allegations.

“For the longest time i thought this was just a funny story to tell at parties when i realized what happened isn’t normal and that he was and could still be doing this to younger girls and it’s my job to say something,” she tweeted. “F*** you @chrisdelia and also @netflix i highly doubt casting this creep as a pedophile was a coincidence. at least i got to see him fake die.”

D’Elia’s latest comedy special, “Chris D’Elia: No Pain,” debuted on Netflix in April. He co-stars in Zack Snyder’s upcoming zombie movie “Army of the Dead,” which filmed in Atlantic City last year.

The comedian’s previous roles include Dean in Freeform’s “Alone Together” and Alex Miller in the NBC comedy “Whitney." He also played Kenny in ABC’s “The Good Doctor" and Bill Stankowski in the TBS series “Glory Daze.”

“Rugby. Simone Rossi, il gemello perduto di Bellini: da metaman a fisioterapista”

Fosse decollata l’Accademia seven della Federazione, il rugby italiano avrebbe un giocatore internazionale in più e un aspirante fisioterapista in meno. Non è successo. Così la coppia di ali più bella del campionato dal Petrarca Padova, nido confortevole dove crescere talenti, ha spiccato il volo in direzioni opposte. Mattia Bellini, 26 anni, corazziere alto 1,93 per 93 chili, è diventato professionista con le Zebre in Pro 14 e star azzurra (25 caps). Simone Rossi, 29 anni, solo tre centimetri più basso, dopo aver toccato il cielo con un dito (scudetto 2018 e metaman del torneo con 11 realizzazioni) ha fatto la scelta opposta. È tornato nella sua città, Milano, a giocare con l’Asr a metà classifica in serie A, allenandosi la sera dopo le otto.

“‘That ’70s Show’ Star Danny Masterson Charged With Sexually Assaulting Three Woman”

Danny Masterson, the star of the hit TV series “That ’70s Show,” has been charged with sexually assaulting three women.

According to the Hollywood Reporter, the Lost Angeles County District Attorney’s office announced that Masterson has been charged with three counts of rape that took place between 2001 and 2003. The actor was arrested by the authorities on Wednesday (June 17) and was taken into custody. However, he was released later in the day on a bond of $3.3 million.

As per the district attorney’s office, the actor is accused of raping a 23-year-old girl in 2001 and a 28-year-old woman in 2003. Masterson is also charged with raping another 23-year-old woman in 2003.

The star’s lawyer came out with a statement and noted that his client is “innocent.” In a statement obtained by Variety, Tom Mesereau, attorney of Masterson, explained that his client would be a free man once the witnesses get an opportunity to testify.

“Mr. Masterson is innocent, and we’re confident that he will be exonerated when all the evidence finally comes to light and witnesses have the opportunity to testify. The people who know Mr. Masterson know his character and know the allegations to be false,” Mesereau said.

This is not the first time that the actor has been accused of sexual assault. In 2019, four women filed a suit against him in the Church of Scientology for harassment. The women alleged that they were being watched by Masterson’s people as they went to the police.

The charges against Masterson came a day after comedian Chris D’Elia was accused of sexual misconduct. Several women took to social media and shared their horrifying encounters with the star.

Simone Rossi released a string of screenshots Tuesday representing a conversation between herself and a person who appears to be D’Elia. Rossi said she was only 16 when D’Elia approached her on the micro-blogging website.

“Imagine being 16 and being groomed by a stand-up comedian twice ur age and the only reason you never met up and never got physically m*lested was because u had just gotten a boyfriend ur own age,” Rossi wrote.

Meanwhile, another user accused the comedian of exposing himself to the hotel staff.

Their tweets went viral and numerous other women joined them with similar allegations against the “You” star, many of them did it anonymously.

“Pallanuoto, nuovo rinnovo in casa Ortigia: Simone Rossi altri due anni in biancoverde”

Il Circolo Canottieri Ortigia comunica ufficialmente di avere rinnovato il rapporto con il difensore Simone Rossi per altri due anni. Simone e l’Ortigia rimarranno ancora insieme fino al 2022.

Il club di via Paolo Caldarella, dunque, continua la sua politica di conferme e rinnovi di quegli atleti che saranno i punti fermi del progetto che, fino al 2024, sarà affidato al tecnico Piccardo. Rossi, che era arrivato lo scorso anno dal Posillipo, è stato autore di una stagione molto positiva, inserendosi perfettamente negli schemi dell’allenatore e nel gruppo. Per lui 20 gol in Serie A1 e un rendimento in continuo crescendo, con ottime prestazioni sia in campionato che in Euro Cup. Il numero 10 biancoverde, che la scorsa estate aveva firmato un accordo annuale, peraltro si è ambientato molto bene a Siracusa, diventando subito uno dei beniamini dei tifosi.

Simone Rossi commenta così il nuovo biennale con l’Ortigia: “Sono davvero contento del rinnovo – afferma – e di aver prolungato il rapporto iniziato lo scorso anno. Sono felicissimo di rimanere qui per altri due anni. Quello che abbiamo fatto l’anno scorso, che non si è concretizzato per le ragioni che ormai sappiamo, sarà il nostro punto di partenza. Da lì dobbiamo ripartire per migliorare ancora. L’obiettivo, ovviamente, a seconda della coppa che giocheremo, se Euro Cup o Champions League, è di arrivare in fondo. Personalmente ho il desiderio di giocare l’Euro Cup per poter provare ad alzare quel maledetto trofeo”.

“In campionato – conclude Simone – il Brescia si è rafforzato ulteriormente, però non mi nascondo e sogno di poter giocare una finale scudetto con il Recco. Nei prossimi due anni mi auguro di arrivare a quei livelli con l’Ortigia e spero che riusciremo a giocarci ogni partita contro queste due formazioni. Questo è l’augurio che faccio a me e alla squadra. Speriamo bene”.

L’Ortigia, dunque, oltre a rimanere vigile sul mercato, riparte dai rinnovi per consolidare un gruppo fortissimo che, malgrado lo stop forzato che ha annullato tutto, in vasca ha disputato una stagione storica.

“Chris D’Elia denies sexual misconduct, pursuing underage women”

Chris D’Elia is denying any wrongdoing in light of accusations that he preyed upon underage women.

“I know I have said and done things that might have offended people during my career, but I have never knowingly pursued any underage women at any point,” D’Elia, 40, told TMZ on Wednesday.

“That being said, I really am truly sorry. I was a dumb guy who ABSOLUTELY let myself get caught up in my lifestyle,” his statement continued. “That’s MY fault. I own it. I’ve been reflecting on this for some time now and I promise I will continue to do better.”

“imagine being 16 and being groomed by a stand up comedian twice ur age and the only reason you never met up and never got physically m*lested was because u had just gotten a boyfriend ur own age,” Rossi wrote alongside screenshots of messages allegedly sent by D’Elia, who once played a pedophile on “Workaholics.”

In response, other women came forward with screenshots of “creepy” conversations with the actor from when they were teenagers, claiming he’d beg for their phone numbers.
