Birthday Post: Ananya Panday Wishes Her Sister Rysa Panday With Cute Childhood Pictures

Bollywood actor Ananya Panday has always been a supportive elder sister be it in advising Rysa or guiding her. The diva is often seen posting pictures with her and is often spotted with her in the city. On Tuesday, Ananya wished Rysa on her sixteenth birthday with a series of adorable childhood pictures of her with Rysa. She wrote, “this brat is finally 16!!! happy bday Rychuuuu – love u the mostest in the whole entire world.” with a heart emoji. As soon as Ananya posted, fans have showered hearts on her adorable post. Ananya Panday is a complete store of talent and fans have gone berserk after watching her movies. She has become a sensation with the success of her second picture “Pati, Patni Aur Woh” where she plays the role of a Delhi-based woman. Take a look: On Women’s Day, Ananya shared a flood of unseen pictures featuring her mommy Bhavana Pandey, sisters Deanne Panday and Alanna Panday and besties Suhana and Shanaya along with their mothers Gauri Khan and Maheep Kapoor respectively. The awwdorable pictures were captioned, “Here’s to the women in my life whose presence is a constant reminder to be courageous, kind and humble #HappyWomensDay to all the beautiful women (sic).” On the professional front, Ananya has been working on her third film – Khaali Peeli with Ishaan Khatter. The makers of Maqbool Khan-directorial, Khaali Peeli, dropped a look featuring Ishaan behind the wheel of a taxi while Ananya occupied the backseat. Looking at her through the rearview mirror, Ishaan had a hint of a smile on his face while Ananya looked tense and fans were on a frenzy to see their chemistry. Khaali Peeli is scheduled to hit the theatre on June 12, 2020. Apart from this, Ananya Panday has begun shooting for her pan-India film, Fighter, opposite South sensation Vijay Devarakonda. Helmed by Puri Jagganadh, it’s an action film with Ananya romancing Vijay in the lead. The actor kick-started the preparations of the film and posted a few pictures of herself with Vijay from the reading session. The movie is bankrolled by Charmie Kaur.
