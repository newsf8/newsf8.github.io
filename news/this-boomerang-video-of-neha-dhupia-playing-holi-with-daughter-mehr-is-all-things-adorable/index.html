THIS boomerang video of Neha Dhupia playing Holi with daughter Mehr is all things adorable

The scare of coronavirus is widespread but little did it do to stop people from coming all out and playing Holi with their family and loved ones. Bollywood celebrities were also no exception to the same. While pictures and videos of celebs celebrating the festival of colours have been making their way into social media, Neha Dhupia ’s adorable post with daughter Mehr caught our attention. She shared a boomerang video of herself with Mehr, where she is seen giving a peck on the cheeks of her bundle of joy as they celebrated Holi.Dressed in black tee and olive coloured pants, Neha is seen cuddling her mini me. Mehr, on the other hand, looks cute as a button as she hugs her mommy while holding on to a water pistol in her hand. Neha captioned the picture as, ‘#happyholi from mine to yours ❤️ @mehrdhupiabedi @babsdhupia ❤️’Meanwhile, on the work front, Neha was last seen in a short film, ‘Devi’ which also starred an ensemble star cast that included, Kajol, Shruti Haasan, Mukta Barve and others in key roles. The short film has been receiving appreciation from all quarters.
