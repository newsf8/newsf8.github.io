4人殭屍射擊《末日Z戰》限期免費，Epic Games本周豪送三款遊戲

2020年3月27日 星期五 上午11:33:08 [台北標準時間]

歪力

就在全球許多遊戲發行商祭出限時免費遊戲以呼籲玩家自宅隔離緩解疫情，在玩家社群評價兩極的 Epic Games 限時免費遊戲活動倒是從未間斷過，如今本周 Epic Games 一口氣送出三款遊戲，其中一款正是賣破 200 萬套的四人制殭屍射擊名作，《末日 Z 戰》（World War Z）。

由 Saber Interactive 開發的《末日 Z 戰》（World War Z）目前在 PC 平台仍為 Epic Games 獨佔，這款被譽為《L4D2》精神續作的殭屍射擊遊戲，玩法幾乎與《L4D2》相同，但也為生存者角色添加了一些獨特的陷阱與特殊技能玩法。如今這款遊戲已在 Epic Games 免費下載，限期一周。

除了《末日 Z 戰》之外，還有獨立好評冒險之作《Figment》，以及充滿動作性與難度的《Tormentor X Punisher》。上述三款遊戲現在都在 Epic Games 展開限時免費，只要登錄帳號領取即可永久保存，時間持續到 4 月 2 日下午 11:00 截止。

如果你從未玩過《末日 Z 戰》，可以參照編輯部先前遊戲實測，再決定要不要領取。

World War Z is free on the Epic Games Store, as cross-play update goes live – TheSixthAxis

From now until 2nd April, you can download and keep the hit co-op zombie shooter World War Z for free on PC viathe Epic Games Store, as Saber Interactive and Focus Home Interactive celebrate the release of a cross-play update to the game.

The update to World War Z enables cross-play between PC and Xbox One, letting you team up to tackle the PvE missions and wave-based Horde Mode Z. A PlayStation 4 update is expected to arrive at some time in the future as well, though there’s no specific timeline on this. Additionally, it seems as though the game’s competitive PvPvE modes are also off limits.

While the big talking point is cross-play, today’s update also adds new weapon variants with special perks, balance improvements for PvE and Horde Mode Z, and four premium character skins that can be bought in a bundle or that come as part of the Season Pass.

Here’s the full patch notes:

New Features

Added Virus Sample functionality in Campaign AI Director will randomly spawn containers with Virus Sample that players can pick up Virus samples can spawn from Bombers that are taken down without them exploding. The containers are fragile and they will begin to apply negative effects to players as they break. If players will manage to finish the mission with containers intact, they will get increased rewards AI Director can spawn several Virus Samples, Bomber will also drop them

Added 4 new weapon variants with unique perks Pistol – Increased penetration and damage vs special zombies. Machine Pistol – Full auto variant, reloads primary weapon after a special zombie kill. Classic Battle Rifle – Semi auto variant, no penetration but deals a lot of damage. Battle Rifle – Full auto variant. Thumper Grenade Launcher – Incendiary grenades.

War Heroes character skin pack (included for Season Pass owners)

Horde Mode

Slightly reduced difficulty in Hard mode

Significantly reduced difficulty in Normal mode Less Special Zombies will spawn in Normal mode Increased preparation timer between waves by 20% in Normal mode Supply points rewards will begin to scale down later in Normal mode Added significantly more random pickups in Normal mode

Increased Auto Turret maximum ammo

Capped by 10 the amount of Auto Turrets that could be placed simultaneously

Fixed one of the shops not spawning items

Reduced the amount of ammo secondary weapons drain from ammo boxes by 50%

Reduced ammo crate cost from 100 to 70

Significantly increased both XP and currency rewards

Difficulty

Tweaked Extreme difficulty settings to restore pre-patch balance (it was changed to support crossplay)

AI Director will spawn less secondary weapon pickups

AI

Reworked Bomber drop, he will spawn better Primary and Heavy weapons when killed

Perks

Fixed several issues with perks stacking

UI

Minor UI and localization fixes

Stability

Fixed several gameplay crashes

Levels

Fixed some gameplay bugs that were causing inability to progress in levels

Fixed issue that allowed players to clip through geometry in some places

PC Specific Fixes

Added crossplay between Xbox in PvE
