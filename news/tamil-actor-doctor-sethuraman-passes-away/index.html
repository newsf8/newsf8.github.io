Tamil actor-doctor Sethuraman passes away

Young Tamil actor Sethuraman, who was also a practising dermatologist, passed away in Chennai after a cardiac arrest. He was 36.

Sethuraman made his debut in Kanna Laddu Thinna Aasaiya alongside Santhanam and Vishaka Singh. He also featured in films like Vaaliba Raja, 50/50 and Sakka Podu Podu Raja. He is survived by his wife and child.
