Infected, Marcelo Magno, presenter of 'Jornal Nacional', talks about the coronavirus

After being diagnosed with coronavirus, Marcelo Magno, presenter of Rede Globo and who participates in the “Jornal Nacional” stand relay, reported on a video last Wednesday (25) the difficult moments he is facing after being infected by the new coronavirus. The video was made by the presenter for “JN”, where he celebrated victory after staying in the ICU for days having to use mechanical ventilation.

Marcelo Magno thanked the prayers and the positive vibrations they have received since his diagnosis and explained that since he started to receive the first symptoms, among them, drowsiness, fever and cough, everything fit the diagnosis of COVID-19.

In addition, the presenter still says that he began to experience such symptoms days after passing through the airport in São Paulo.

After the first symptoms, Marcelo says he stopped at the ICU, but soon after he was sent to the semi-ICU. Magno says he had the support of health professionals and, now, fortunately, he is already in his apartment, strictly following all the treatment, however, still isolated. “Celebrating a victory,” he said.

After Marcelo Magno’s video was broadcast, Renata Vasconcellos confessed that the news was “a relief”.

It is worth remembering that on March 7, the journalist was in Rio de Janeiro and São Paulo to present the “Jornal Nacional”.

Diagnosis

Marcelo Magno was only diagnosed with the new coronavirus, five days after being admitted to the ICU of a hospital, where he stayed for a few days under mechanical ventilation. However, since last Tuesday (23), the presenter is already breathing without the aid of devices.

Your Waldemar tests positive for COVID-19

TV presenter Anhanguera Waldemar Neto, better known in the artistic world as Seu Waldemar, was also diagnosed with a new coronavirus this Wednesday (25). The information was released by the family of the presenter of “No Balaio”, which airs every Saturday at Rede Globo’s affiliate. After the diagnosis, family members inform that the presenter remains hospitalized in the ICU (Intensive Care Unit).

In addition, the presenter’s relatives also say that he is responding positively to the procedures that are being performed and, even according to his family members, it is also possible to notice a considerable improvement in clinical examinations. It is worth mentioning that, initially, Seu Waldemar was diagnosed with pneumonia, however, even so, tests were performed on COVID-19, which confirmed that the presenter was also infected by the virus.

Marcelo Magno deixa UTI e aparece em vídeo ao lado de equipe médica

Foi divulgado na tarde desta quarta-feira (25) o novo boletim médico do jornalista Marcelo Magno, diagnosticado com Covid-19. O quadro do apresentador do Piauí TV 1 segue estável e ele deixou a UTI na tarde de hoje. No vídeo acima, ele aparece ao lado da equipe médica que o acompanha.

Conforme o boletim, Marcelo manteve um bom padrão respiratório e sem o aporte de oxigênio suplementar. Ele foi transferido da UTI para o apartamento após confirmação de quadro clínico estável e sem intercorrências.

O apresentador foi diagnosticado com a Covid-19 no último dia 19, quando retornou de uma viagem ao Rio de Janeiro após apresentar o Jornal Nacional.

Qualquer nova informação oficial sobre o estado de saúde de Marcelo Magno será divulgada nos jornais da TV Clube e no G1 Piauí.

Diagnosticado com Covid-19, Marcelo Magno segue internado em UTI e apresenta melhora

O boletim médico divulgado neste domingo (22) apontou uma evolução positiva no quadro de saúde do jornalista Marcelo Magno, que permanece internado na Unidade de Terapia Intensiva (UTI) de um hospital particular de Teresina . Na quinta-feira (19), o apresentador foi diagnosticado com coronavírus .

Segundo os médicos, as últimas radiografias mostraram que o apresentador teve melhoras em suas funções pulmonares. A pressão arterial de Marcelo foi considerada dentro da normalidade.

Ainda segundo o boletim médico, Marcelo apresenta boa troca respiratória e boas funções renais, e está sem febre.
