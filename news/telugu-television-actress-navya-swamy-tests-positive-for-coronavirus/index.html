Telugu television actress Navya Swamy tests positive for coronavirus

Earlier this month, Telangana government brought some relief to the Telugu film and television industry by relaxing lockdown restrictions and allowing them to resume shooting from June 15.

Following this, many actors began shooting for television serials adhering to rules imposed by the state and the central government.

According to the latest reports, popular small-screen actress Navya Swamy has tested positive for coronavirus. Sources say that it was only during the shoot of her Telugu serial, Naa Peru Meenakshi, the actress underwent coronavirus test and the result turned out to be positive. She is currently in self-quarantine.

The actress, who has been part of many television soaps, including Vani Rani, Aranmanai Kili, Run and Aame Katha, is apparently asymptomatic and is confident of recovering soon. The makers of the serial and her co-stars are helping her out to cope with the situation.

Meanwhile, other artistes, who came in contact with Navya Swamy during the shoot, have been requested to isolate themselves.

Another television actor Boddu Prabhakar was recently tested positive and is currently under treatement.

“Karthika Deepam: Hima’s declaration leaves Karthik worried”

“Bigg Boss Telugu 3 fame Ravikrishna and Navya Swamy starrer ‘Amekatha’ to premiere tonight”

The new Telugu fiction daily titled “Amekatha” is all set to premiere tonight (November 4).Aame Katha, starring Ravikrishna of Bigg Boss Telugu 3 fame, Navya Swamy and Shisha in the lead roles, is touted to be an edge of the seat thriller.Going by the teaser of the show, Gautham (Ravikrishna), a well off young man, deceitfully marries Aishwarya (played Navya Swamy), a kind-hearted woman who appreciates honesty. In yet another teaser, he introduces her to his family and even requests to change her name for him. The show will showcase Aishwarya’s quest for the truth in the ocean of lies and deceit.Speaking about Aame Katha , Alok Jain, Business Head of the channel airing the show, said, “We have always experimented with stories that have an ability to challenge the status quo. Amekatha is a very powerful story of an ordinary girl who will face an extraordinary challenge. What starts like a fairy-tale wedding turns out to be a web of deceit.”While the former Dance Jodi Dance actor shot to fame with his performances in daily soaps like Varudhini Parinayam , Bavamaradallu, That is Mahalakshmi, Navya Swamy has featured in popular shows like Vaani Rani.

“Sarah Annaiah goes grey for Kannada serial debut”

Namoora Haiklu actress Sarah Annaiah, who had moved to the small screen with the Tamil soap opera Malar, is now coming to Kannada TV. The actress, we hear, has been signed up for Kannadathi , which has Kiran Raj and Ranjani Raghavan in the lead. “While I would have loved to start my small screen tryst with a Kannada serial, the opportunity first came from Tamil. I am glad that I have been offered a role in Kannadathi; better late than never, right?” she says.In Kannadathi, Sarah will play Varudhini, a wedding planner who hails from an affluent family. “Interestingly, my character also has a negative shade, which will be revealed later on in the narrative. Varudhini is a straightforward girl, who comes across as rather rude, but manages to justify her behaviour. The extent to which she goes about planning things to make sure she gets what she wants will intrigue viewers,” says the actress, who adds that she is set to sign up for another Kannada film as well and that she is looking at balancing careers on the big and small screen.
