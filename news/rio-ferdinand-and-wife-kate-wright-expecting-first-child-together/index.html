Rio Ferdinand and wife Kate Wright expecting first child together

Rio Ferdinand and wife Kate Wright have announced that they are expecting their first child together.

Revealing the happy news on Instagram, the couple shared a video of Ferdinand’s three children celebrating after being told their step-mother was expecting.

“We got our wish, another Ferdinand on route!!” the former England defender, 41, wrote.

“I can’t wait to go through this whole pregnancy together and then bring our little human into this world.”

In her own post, reality star Wright, 29, said: “The crazy house is about to get a little bit crazier.

“Every time I watch this it makes me cry, the best reaction. Baby Ferdy, we are all so excited to meet you.”

The news of the pregnancy comes nine months after the couple tied the knot in September last year with a ceremony in Turkey.

They have been in a relationship since 2017 and got engaged a year later with the help of Ferdinand’s three children - Lorenz, 13, Tate, 11, and Tia, nine - from his marriage with his late wife, Rebecca Ellison.

Ellison died in 2015 after being diagnosed with breast cancer for a second time. She was aged 34.

Image: The news comes months after they tied the knot in September last year

Among the stars to congratulate the couple on their news on Thursday was several of Wright’s former co-stars on The Only Way is Essex.

Georgia Kousoulou wrote in the comments: “Aww this is so special,” while Ferne McCann added: “Oh wow this is amazing, congratulations.”

Ferdinand also received congratulations from the sporting world on his post, with comments from Middlesbrough manager Jonathan Woodgate and Newcastle player Jacob Murphy.

“Rio Ferdinand drops transfer hint amid Manchester United’s Saul Niguez pursuit”

Rio Ferdinand has hinted that Manchester United could be eyeing a move for Spanish star Saul Niguez.

The Atletico Madrid star has been tipped for a high profile move this summer with a switch to Old Trafford one of a number of options.

The 25-year-old has a release clause of £135million and could be tempted by a move to one of the worlds’ biggest clubs.

Niguez sparked excitement in the football world when he took to Twitter to tease fans over a potential move by posting: “New club, I’ll announce it in three days”.

It remains to be seen what the announcement will be, with some suggesting that his ‘new club’ could be a reference to the world of esports.

But that hasn’t stopped Ferdinand from sparking excitement among the Manchester United fans.

Retweeting Saul Niguez’s post, Ferdinand simply posted a pair of eyes to indicate that supporters should watch this space.

Manchester United supporters will certainly be hoping for a busy summer in the transfer market as Ole Gunnar Solskjaer looks to bolster his squad.

Several high profile signings could be needed to put themselves back in contention for the Premier League title, especially with Paul Pogba’s future up in the air.

The French midfielder has regularly been linked with a move away from Old Trafford, and it seems likely that a final decision will be made on his future in the North West over the summer.

GIVEMESPORT’S Phil Spencer says…

While a move for Saul Niguez is far from close, it could be exactly what fans have been waiting for at Old Trafford.

The midfielder is a hugely talented individual with experience of playing at the highest level - something which Manchester United could benefit from.

A deal - should it be on the cards - would be a huge financial commitment though, and that could be a potential stumbling block, especially with their place in next season’s Champions League in doubt.

With the Premier League season set to resume in the coming weeks, United are currently in fifth place in the table and three points behind Chelsea in fourth.

That could play a big part in their hopes of signing players such as Saul Niguez.
